EDG's Glorious History

  1. 2013:
    • September - Edward Gaming was founded by former World Elite members and began competing in the League of Legends scene.
  2. 2014:
    • Spring & Summer - Won both the LPL Spring and Summer Splits.
    • October - Competed in their first World Championship but were eliminated in the quarterfinals.
  3. 2015:
    • Spring - Won the LPL Spring Split.
    • May - Triumphed at the Mid-Season Invitational (MSI), defeating SK Telecom T1 in the final.
    • Summer - Secured another LPL Summer Split title.
    • October - Advanced to the quarterfinals of the World Championship but were defeated by Fnatic.
  4. 2016:
    • Spring & Summer - Continued their domestic success by winning both the Spring and Summer LPL Splits.
    • October - Reached the quarterfinals of the World Championship, losing to ROX Tigers.
  5. 2017:
    • Spring - Won the LPL Spring Split.
    • Summer - Finished in second place in the Summer Split.
    • October - Did not advance past the group stage at the World Championship.
  6. 2018:
    • Spring - Placed second in the Spring LPL.
    • Summer - Won the LPL Summer Split.
    • November - Won their first League of Legends World Championship by defeating Fnatic in the finals.
  7. 2019:
    • Spring & Summer - Experienced a decline, failing to win any titles but managed to qualify for the World Championship.
    • October - Exited the World Championship in the group stage.
  8. 2020:
    • Spring & Summer - Did not claim any LPL titles and did not qualify for the World Championship.
  9. 2021:
    • Spring - Finished third in the LPL Spring Split.
    • Summer - Won the LPL Summer Split.
    • October - Reached the semifinals of the World Championship, losing to DWG KIA.
  10. 2022:
    • Spring & Summer - Competed in the LPL but did not claim any titles.
    • Did not qualify for the World Championship.
